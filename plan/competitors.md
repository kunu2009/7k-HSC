## ğŸ” Gap Analysis

### What Competitors Are Missing

1. **Maharashtra HSC Specialization**
   - Most apps are generic or CBSE-focused
   - No app has complete MH State Board syllabus
   - Paper patterns not addressed properly

2. **Scientific Study Methods**
   - No competitor uses spaced repetition for HSC
   - Active recall not emphasized
   - Passive reading/video watching dominant

3. **Student-Centric Pricing**
   - Major apps are prohibitively expensive
   - Quality content locked behind paywalls
   - No sustainable free model

4. **AI Integration**
   - Limited AI doubt solving
   - No AI-generated content
   - No personalized learning paths

5. **Micro-Learning**
   - No "reels" style quick learning
   - Content always in long format
   - Not optimized for short attention spans
